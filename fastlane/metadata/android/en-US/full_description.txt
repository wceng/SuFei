SuFei (素扉) is a minimalist application dedicated to the immersive reading of traditional Chinese poetry. Designed with the philosophy of "Simple Elegance" (素) and "Opening the Door to Literature" (扉), it provides a digital space free from advertisements and social distractions.

Key Features:
<ul>
  <li><b>Immersive Reading</b>: A refined layout optimized for classical texts, supporting built-in high-quality serif fonts (Noto Serif SC) and a traditional paper-like reading feel across all Android versions.</li>
  <li><b>Daily Encounters</b>: Discover a curated piece of poetry every hour. Our smart algorithm extracts the most evocative and semantically complete lines, presented in a beautiful vertical layout.</li>
  <li><b>Comprehensive Library</b>: Browse through a vast collection with a structured filtering system by dynasty, tune name (Ci-pai), and thematic tags.</li>
  <li><b>Personal Collection</b>: Save your favorite pieces to your "Bedside" collection for quiet reflection at any time.</li>
  <li><b>Genre Awareness</b>: Intelligent recognition of different styles (Shi, Ci, Qu), automatically tailoring the extraction and display logic to capture the soul of each genre.</li>
  <li><b>Modern Experience</b>: Fully built with Jetpack Compose and Navigation 3, featuring fluid crossfade transitions and native support for Android 13+ predictive back gestures.</li>
</ul>
Experience the eternal beauty of classical Chinese literature in its purest form with SuFei.
