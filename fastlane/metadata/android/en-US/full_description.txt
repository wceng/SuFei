SuFei (素扉) is a minimalist application dedicated to the immersive reading of traditional Chinese poetry. Designed with the philosophy of "Simple Elegance" (素) and "Opening the Door to Literature" (扉), it provides a digital space free from advertisements and social distractions.

Key Features:

- **Immersive Reading**: A refined layout optimized for classical texts, supporting built-in high-quality serif fonts (Noto Serif SC) and a traditional paper-like reading feel across all Android versions.
- **Daily Encounters**: Discover a curated piece of poetry every hour. Our smart algorithm extracts the most evocative and semantically complete lines, presented in a beautiful vertical layout.
- **Comprehensive Library**: Browse through a vast collection with a structured filtering system by dynasty, tune name (Ci-pai), and thematic tags.
- **Personal Collection**: Save your favorite pieces to your "Bedside" collection for quiet reflection at any time.
- **Genre Awareness**: Intelligent recognition of different styles (Shi, Ci, Qu), automatically tailoring the extraction and display logic to capture the soul of each genre.
- **Modern Experience**: Fully built with Jetpack Compose and Navigation 3, featuring fluid crossfade transitions and native support for Android 13+ predictive back gestures.

Experience the eternal beauty of classical Chinese literature in its purest form with SuFei.
